More Info on Pink Eye

It is the inflammation or infection of the outer membrane of the eyeball and inner eyelid. It can spread easily and is short-term, which means that it can be resolved within days to weeks. It is advised that you not wear contact lenses while affected by pink eye, which is also known as conjunctivitis.

How does it spread?

It can spread by skin-to-skin contact and by touching a contaminated surface.
