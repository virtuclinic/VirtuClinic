More Info On Common Colds

It is a common viral infection of the throat and nose. It is usually self-treatable and self-diagnosable. Common colds are very short-term ailments and can spread easily. There are many types of viruses that can cause one to have a common cold.

How does it spread?

It can spread by airborne respiratory droplets (which is by coughing or sneezing), by saliva, by skin-to-skin contact and by touching a contaminated surface.

Information on Treatments!

Most people, within two weeks, can recover on their own. One soothing remedy is to apply menthol to the affected area (for example, you could use Vicks VapoRub). A pain reliever that one could use to treat their common cold is acetaminophen , or Tylenol. Some other treatments include Mucinex, Delsym, and Zicam. You can also use a throat lozenge to sooth your throat. Two other methods to assuage your symptoms are cough medicine and nasal irrigation.
