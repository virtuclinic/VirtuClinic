More Info on the Flu

It is a common viral infection that can spread easily, is partly preventable by vaccination, and can be resolved within days to weeks (short-term).

How does it spread?

It can spread by airborne respiratory droplets (which is by coughing or sneezing), by saliva, by skin-to-skin contact and by touching a contaminated surface.

Information on Treatments!

Most of the time, people can recover from the flu on their own. Some methods of treatment include:

  • Peramivir by injection
  • Zanamivir by inhaling
  • Oseltamivir
  • Ibuprofen
  • Acetaminophen (Tylenol)
  • Guaifenesin (Mucinex)
  • Dextromethorphan (Delsym)
  • Pseudoephedrine (Sudafed)
  • Fluid replacement
  • Bed rest
  • Throat lozenge
  • Cough medicine
