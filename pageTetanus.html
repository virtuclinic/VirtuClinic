More Info On Tetanus

It is an extremely rare disease, where a puncture can lead to infection. It is preventable by vaccine, and is medium-term, meaning that it can resolve within months.

How does it spread?

It can spread by touching a contaminated surface.

Information on Treatments!

The treatments mostly focus on managing complications. Some prescriptions given include penicillin (an antibiotic), Metronidazole by injection, Diazepam by injection , mechanical ventilation, tracheotomy , and the removal of unhealthy tissue.
