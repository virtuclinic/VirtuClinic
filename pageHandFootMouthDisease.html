More Info on Hand Foot Mouth Disease

It is a common children's virus, and it can cause rashes on the hands and feet, as well as sores in the mouth. It can be resolved within days to weeks, as it is short-term. It can also spread easily.

How does it spread?

It can spread by airborne respiratory droplets (which is by coughing or sneezing), by saliva, and by skin-to-skin contact.
