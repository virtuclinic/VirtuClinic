More Info on Whooping Cough

It is a rare and highly contagious respiratory tract infection, but is easily preventable by vaccinations. It is short-term, meaning that it can be resolved within days to weeks. This illness is even more dangerous for infants.

How does it spread?

It can spread by airborne respiratory droplets (which is by coughing or sneezing), by saliva, and by skin-to-skin contact.

Information on Treatments!

Some prescriptions include:

  • Clarithromycin (Biaxin)
  • Erythromycin (Ery-tab)
  • Azithromycin (Zithromax)
  • monitoring for changes/improvement
