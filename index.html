Let's Get to Diagnosing!

This web application will help diagnose you if you believe that you have a common illness or sickness. You will answer a series of questions and at the end, you will be provided some information and steps to treat your illness. However, if you are in pain, it is strongly recommended that you get help! One way you can get help (if you are in an emergency) is to dial 911! Enjoy!

Proceed onwards if you believe that you have one of the following illnesses:

  • a common cold
  • Tetanus
  • a Fever
  • the flu
  • Meningitis
  • an Ear Infection
  • Whooping Cough
  • Pink Eye
  • Chicken Pox
  • Hand Foot Mouth disease
